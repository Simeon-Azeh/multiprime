Why we started?

The ALU Alumni Connect Hub is a dedicated platform that informs you about upcoming events, networking opportunities, and important updates from your university community.

Vision

We aim to build AlumniConnect into the go-to platform for all alumni-related activities, facilitating meaningful connections and enhancing the overall alumni experience.

Mission

Our mission is to create a vibrant and supportive network for alumni to reconnect with old friends, forge new connections, and access opportunities for continued learning and development.

Goals

We aim to cultivate a vibrant and supportive community of engaged alumni, fostering lifelong learning,and professional growth while strengthening connections with the university and celebrating alumni achievements.
